Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse restaurants, select your items, and proceed to checkout. You can pay online or choose cash on delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, mobile payments, and cash on delivery. All online payments are securely processed.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking link. You can also track orders in the "My Orders" section of your account.

What's your delivery time?

Delivery times vary by restaurant and location, but most orders arrive within 30-45 minutes. You'll see estimated times during checkout.
